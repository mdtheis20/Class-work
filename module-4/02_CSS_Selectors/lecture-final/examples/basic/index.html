Paragraphs are embedded within the "p" element, and are usually long lines of text. Line feeds and white-space in the document are not necessarily reflected when rendered by the browser. Instead, the browser figures out how to flow the paragraph based on the user's screen. So type however you want to in order to look nice in your html editor, and it will look fine in the browser, too.
